About Us

Gonzalez Refrigeration & Heating, LLC. was founded in 2020. We proudly serve the San Antonio area and surrounding counties which include residential and commercial properties. We are certified and licensed to work on all major heating and cooling systems.

Gonzalez Refrigeration & Heating strives to make integrity our main priority every day. Our reputation as a trustworthy and dependable HVAC service provider means more to us than anything. We have never believed in using gimmicks to get sales but rely on our honesty to create long lasting customer relationships.
